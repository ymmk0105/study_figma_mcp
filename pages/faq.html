Songs & Languages

Songs & Language Support

Yes! Manekineko offers an extensive library of English songs, as well as songs in Korean, Chinese, and many other languages.

Yes! The karaoke system supports searching in English, Japanese, Korean (Hangul), and Chinese (both Traditional and Simplified). You can type directly in your native script.

Many of our popular tourist locations have English-speaking staff. Translation assistance is also available via our app and in-room tablets.

Reservations & Pricing

Reservations & Pricing

Yes, you can make reservations online through our website or by calling the store directly. Walk-ins are also welcome based on room availability.

There is typically a minimum 30-minute session. Pricing varies by time of day and store location.

Karaoke Manekineko charges per person per hour. Daytime plans (before 18:00) start from ¥330/person. Night plans (18:00 onwards) start from ¥550/person. All-night plans are available Friday and Saturday from midnight to morning from ¥1,650/person.

Yes, children are welcome. Children under a certain age may be eligible for discounted rates. Please inquire at the store for family pricing details.

Payment & Access

Payment & Access

Most Manekineko locations accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex). Some locations also accept IC cards and QR payment (PayPay, Alipay, WeChat Pay). Cash in Japanese Yen is also accepted.

There is no dress code at Karaoke Manekineko. Come as you are — casual attire is perfectly fine.

Services

E-bo & Raibo

E-bo is Manekineko's in-room entertainment system. It lets you play console games, watch live sports, use VR, and control the room from your smartphone — all in addition to karaoke.

Raibo is Manekineko's live viewing system. It broadcasts live concerts, sports events, and anime screenings directly to your private karaoke room on a large display.
