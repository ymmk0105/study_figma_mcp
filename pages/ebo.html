E-bo Entertainment System

The ultimate in-room entertainment experience

E-bo transforms your karaoke room into a full entertainment hub. Play console-quality games, watch sports events live, and control everything from your smartphone.

Play popular video games right in your karaoke room with the latest gaming consoles.

⚽

📱

How It Works

How to Use E-bo

1

Book an E-bo Room

Reserve a room with E-bo when booking online or at the front desk. Ask staff for an "E-bo room" (E-boルーム).

2

Connect Your Smartphone

Scan the QR code in the room with your smartphone to connect. No app download required — just use your browser.

3

Enjoy & Sing

Switch between gaming, karaoke, and sports streaming whenever you like. Order food and drinks from the same interface.
